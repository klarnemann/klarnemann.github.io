About Me

It's hard to escape the city, but when I do I spend as much time as possible hiking and exploring the great outdoors. I appreciate the awe-inspiring complexity of nature, which I enjoy documenting through photography. I also enjoy the meditation and delious reward of cooking. And, while my BA in philsophy and cognitive science may be a few years behind me, from time to time I still love to delve into lifes great mysteries.
